Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about library services, accounts, and books.

Creating an account is simple! Click on the 'Login' button located at the top right of the homepage. Select "New User Registration" and follow the on-screen instructions. You will need your student ID card for verification.

You can use the main search bar on the homepage to find books. Our system supports searching by Title, Author, or ISBN. For advanced filtering (like genre or publication year), visit the "Catalog" page.

The Central Library is open from 10:30 AM to 9:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. The reading halls remain open 24/7 during exam periods. We are closed on public holidays.

Once you are logged in, navigate to the 'Browse Books' section. Select the book you wish to borrow and click 'Reserve' or 'Check Out'. Collect the physical copy from the issue counter within 24 hours.

To view your history and current borrowings, go to 'My Dashboard' after logging in. This section shows due dates, fines (if any), and allows you to renew books online.
